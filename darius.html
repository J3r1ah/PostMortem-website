Moveset Overview

UNIQUE MOVES

Noxian Revolution

Darius impales the opponent on the head of his axe, leaps high into the air, and slams them down behind him. This can hit opponents that are knocked down.

Noxian Might

Darius slashes across the screen, knocking the opponent back.

Noxian Guillotine (Ultimate)

Darius winds up his axe for a powerful swing that carries his opponent upwards. While in air, Darius strikes multiple times before smashing to the ground with an overhead slam. A fully invincible attack that deals massive damage.

Wound

Darius Wounds his opponents by hitting them with the blade of his axe on Sunder and Lacerate. While Wounded, opponents take extra block damage, even from normals. Hitting Darius or his teammate removes this debuff.
