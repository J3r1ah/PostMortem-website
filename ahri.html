AHRI

THE NINE-TAILED FOX

A Tier

Ahri is a fox-like vastaya who is as graceful as she is powerful. Difficult to defend against and harder to pin down, she’s a mobile mage with a focus on precision skillshots and tons of pressure tools at her disposal.

She’s an A teir character that is fast and movement based. Ahri is a character with tons of abilities to apply crazy pressure and mix up potential def one of the better characters in 2XKO.

Input Overview

Unique Moves

Orb of Deception

Ahri fires an orb that hits the opponent multiple times before exploding. On whiff, the orb will try to return to Ahri.

When S1 is held, Ahri fires an orb that attempts to return to her up to two times. She can pass through it using Spirit Rush.

Hyper Spirit Rush

Ahri rushes forward, damaging enemies in her path. Ahri can steer this move.

Charm (Ultimate)

Ahri blows a kiss at her opponent, unleashing an invincible projectile that leaves them defenseless. She channels a forceful burst of spirit magic, blasting her target across the screen.

Air Dash

Ahri dashes forward or backward in the air.

Chromas

Ahri has 10 different chromas available.
