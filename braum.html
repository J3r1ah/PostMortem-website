Braum

THE HEART OF THE FRELJORD

B Tier

Braum roams the frostbitten wilds of the Freljord, lending his shield to those in need. He deploys defensive utility, close-range attacks, and an army of poros to protect his allies and disrupt enemies.

Moveset Overview

UNIQUE MOVES

Glacial Fissure

Braum performs a massive jump, crashing down to the ground with his shield and sending out a wave of ice.

The Heart of the Freljord

Braum slams an enemy with his shield in an armored attack, activating Unbreakable. If Unbreakable is already active, it’s consumed to deal more damage

Snax Time (Ultimate)

Braum calls his fluffy friends for snack time. A herd of poros stampedes through the stage, overwhelming Braum’s opponent. A fully invincible attack that deals massive damage.

Unbreakable

Glacier Breaker, Iceborn Smash, and Heart of the Freljord can activate Unbreakable. While in Unbreakable, Braum gains one hit of armor on his shield normals and improved special moves. Braum loses Unbreakable when thrown and after using certain special moves.
