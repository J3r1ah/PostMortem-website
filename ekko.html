Ekko

THE BOY WHO SHATTERED TIME

S tier

A prodigy from the rough streets of Zaun, Ekko manipulates time to twist any situation to his advantage. Using his own invention, the Z-Drive, he thrives in pressure mixups and burst mobility.

Moveset Overview

Unique Moves

Mega Timewinder

Ekko throws a quickly-traveling projectile that hits multiple times before returning.

Ekko can bat Mega Timewinder with his heavies.

Tangled Timeline

Ekko creates a Volatile Afterimage, which he can rewind to multiple times. It persists for several seconds.

Attempt #37 (Ultimate)

Ekko activates the Z-Drive, dashing to his opponent and slowing time to strike them.

If he’s hit during startup, he’ll rewind time to dodge and punish his opponent.

Afterimage

Ekko’s S2 specials leave behind Afterimages, allowing him to rewind time and jump back to them. Ekko can rewind to standard Afterimages to perform a strike. He can hold S2 to rewind to a Volatile Afterimage and perform any action, or emit a powerful burst.
