Yasuo

THE UNFORGIVEN

B Tier

An Ionian of deep resolve, Yasuo is an agile swordsman who wields the wind itself against his enemies. He excels at mid-range, using the power of his wind technique to dash through opponents and block projectiles.

Moveset Overview

Unique Moves

Storm Breaker

Yasuo dashes through his opponent, striking them many times

Rise, With the Wind

Yasuo summons an enormous tornado to launch his opponent into the air. Holding Up + S1 + S2 spends two more bars of meter and cancels into Last Breath.

Last Breath (Ultimate)

Yasuo performs a rising slash that carries the opponent through the air. He strikes them multiple times while airborne, then dashes through them to the ground. Yasuo lands behind his opponent in a gale of wind.

Calm

Yasuo sheaths his sword to enter a Calm stance from which he can perform powerful, long-range slashes. He can cancel into this stance from any special attack.
